Welcome to Kinvara Inn B&B

Eileen and John Connolly’s family-friendly guest house is located on a small hill beside a mature evergreen tree plantation, overlooking green fields which transform into a lake during the winter.

This high setting provides magnificent views of the Burren and surrounding countryside.

One of Kinvara’s more attractive accommodations, the house is beautifully appointed. All rooms are spacious and have modern bathrooms.

Your Gateway to the Wild Atlantic Way

  • Shannon Airport: 45 mins
  • Cliffs of Moher: 55 mins
  • Galway City: 45 mins
  • Connemara: 55 mins
